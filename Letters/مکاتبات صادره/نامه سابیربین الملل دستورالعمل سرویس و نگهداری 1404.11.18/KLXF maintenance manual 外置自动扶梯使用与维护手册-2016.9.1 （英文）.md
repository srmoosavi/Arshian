passengers in different floor of the buildings.

1）Evenly and continually transportation equipment for passenger with big capacity.
2）Can be operated upwards or downwards.

3.6、Mesh and protection of comb plate and step
3.6.1、In order to assure safety and prevent the clamping of object between step and floor
plate during the operation of the escalator, the comb plate is installed in the front end of floor
plate. It is fixed on the comb by screw for ease of replacement. The comb plate is made of
plastic or aluminum alloy. The comb teeth exactly mesh with the groove on the step surface.
梯
Even if the feet of the pedestrian or the article remains still on the step, they will be smoothly
transited to the comb plate.
电
3.6.2、When the step is meshed with the comb plate through the comb, the mesh depth of the
combs into the grooves of the tread shall be at least 4mm, the clearance shall not exceed
4mm.
力
3.6.3、During the step operation, check if each step is correctly meshed when it passes the
comb plate. The teeth of the comb plate shall be in the middle of the groove of the step pedal
康
to avoid friction between tooth and groove.
3.6.4、In order to prevent the steps from entering the comb teeth to cause step damage due to
clamping of hard object, a comb tooth foreign matter protection device is installed under the
Y
floor plate. When the umbrella tip, the heel of the high heels or other foreign matter of the
passenger is inserted inN the place where the step and comb teeth are meshed, the comb plate
will be pushed and the comb plate will push the floor plate to move and strike the
micro-switch. TNhe escalator will immediately stop running.
3.6.5、The step is the moving part for bearing the passengers. It is very dangerous if the step
A

escalator; when the step is broken and sunk to touch the upright of the protection switch, t the
